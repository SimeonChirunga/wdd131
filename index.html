Gweru, Zimbabwe

Gweru, Zimbabwe's Midlands Province capital, is a vibrant city with a rich history dating back to 1894. Founded as a mining town, Gweru boasts a strong economy, driven by agriculture, mining, and manufacturing. Visitors can explore the Midlands Museum, Gweru Antelope Game Park, and the historic Boggie Clock Tower, while enjoying the city's mild climate. Gweru is also a hub for education, home to reputable institutions like Midlands State University and Thornhill High School.
