Admission Process and Requirements

To apply to Thornhill High School, please follow these steps:

  1. Submit an application form
  2. Provide transcripts and test scores
  3. Attend an interview

Requirements:

  • Age: 14-18 years old
  • Residency: Must reside in the school district
  • Academic record: Must have a good academic record
