School History

Thornhill High School was founded in 1960 with a mission to provide quality education to the community.

Over the years, the school has grown and evolved to meet the changing needs of its students and the community.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to empower students to succeed in academics, arts, and athletics, and to become responsible citizens in a rapidly changing world.
